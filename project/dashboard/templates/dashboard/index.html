Melbourne's Antique Destination

Located in the heart of Melbourne, Victoria, Edwards Emporium brings together carefully curated pieces from flea markets and antique fairs around the world. Each item is selected for its history, craftsmanship, and timeless appeal.

Whether you're a seasoned collector or beginning your journey into antiques, we invite you to explore our collection and discover pieces that resonate with you.
